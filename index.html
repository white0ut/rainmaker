Kenosha Fishing Seasons

While fishing is great all season, here’s a general guide to peak times for target species:

Spring (Apr-May)

Hot Bite: Brown Trout, Coho Salmon (peak May), Steelhead (peak May)

Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot Bite: Coho Salmon, Lake Trout, Steelhead, King Salmon (start July, peak Aug)

Fall (Sep-Oct)

Hot Bite: King Salmon (Trophy Time!), Steelhead (Oct), Brown Trout (Oct)

Remember: Our captains are on the water daily and adapt to find the most active fish, regardless of the calendar!

Anglers aged 16 and over need a Wisconsin fishing license and a Great Lakes Salmon/Trout stamp. You can buy these online beforehand via the WI DNR website, or purchase a one-day license directly on the boat ($14 for WI, $6 for IL if we cross the state line).
